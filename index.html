Privacy Settings

1. Introduction

Welcome to Global Weather App ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to maintaining the privacy and security of your personal information while providing you with accurate weather forecasts.

App Functionality

Our app provides real-time weather forecasts, favorite city storage, and personalized weather alerts based on your preferences.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide

  • Favorite Cities: When you save cities to your favorites, we store them locally on your device.
  • Settings Preferences: Your app settings (temperature unit, dark mode, etc.) are stored locally.
  • Location Data: Only if you enable "Auto Location" in settings.

2.2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Weather Data Requests: When you search for cities or view weather information.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality.
Data Collection Details

We collect minimal data to provide you with the best weather experience. All data is processed locally on your device whenever possible.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide accurate weather forecasts and related services
  • Remember your favorite cities and preferences
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Personalize content based on your settings
  • Send weather alerts and notifications (if enabled)

4. Third-Party Services

4.1. Open-Meteo API

  • We use the Open-Meteo API to fetch weather data
  • When you search for a city, your search query is sent to Open-Meteo
  • Open-Meteo's privacy policy applies to their data collection practices
  • We do not control how Open-Meteo uses your search queries

4.2. Analytics Services

We may use anonymous analytics to understand app usage patterns and improve functionality. No personally identifiable information is collected.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1. Local Storage

  • All your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
  • We do not have access to this data
  • Data persists on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data

5.2. Security Measures

  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information
  • However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure
  • We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1. Access and Control

  • You can view, edit, or delete your favorite cities within the app
  • You can change your settings at any time
  • You can disable location services in your device settings

6.2. Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply uninstall the app. All locally stored data will be removed from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
